Design Philosophy





As a child, I was always fascinated and drawn to design, whether it be from a magazine, poster, shirt, or the packaging of a brand new toy. How the design would catch my eye and invoke emotions always interested me, and that's what I want to happen when people look at my designs. I personally believe that art and design, whether people notice it or not, always have that affect on people. I pursue design so I can make that impact on others the same way it made an impact on me.
